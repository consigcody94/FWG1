Cody Churchwell is the Chief Executive Officer of Federal Working Group, bringing over 15 years of experience in federal IT consulting and technology leadership. Under his guidance, FWG has grown to become a trusted partner for federal agencies seeking innovative IT solutions.

## Leadership & Vision

Cody's vision for FWG focuses on delivering cutting-edge technology solutions while maintaining the highest standards of security and compliance. His deep understanding of federal requirements and commitment to excellence has positioned FWG as a leader in the federal IT services market.

## Expertise

- **Strategic IT Planning**: Developing comprehensive IT strategies aligned with agency missions
- **Federal Compliance**: Deep knowledge of FedRAMP, FISMA, and NIST frameworks
- **Team Building**: Building high-performing teams of certified professionals
- **Client Relations**: Maintaining strong partnerships with federal agencies

Cody holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and multiple industry certifications including PMP and CISSP.
