## Position Overview

Federal Working Group is seeking a skilled Network Engineer to design, implement, and maintain mission-critical network infrastructure for federal agencies. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience with enterprise networking, strong security knowledge, and a commitment to supporting critical government operations.

As a Network Engineer at FWG, you'll work with cutting-edge technologies while ensuring the highest levels of security, reliability, and performance for federal networks that support important government missions.
