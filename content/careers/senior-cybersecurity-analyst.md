## Position Overview

Federal Working Group is seeking an experienced Senior Cybersecurity Analyst to join our team supporting critical federal agencies. This role offers the opportunity to work on high-impact security initiatives protecting national interests.

As a Senior Cybersecurity Analyst, you'll lead security monitoring, incident response, and compliance efforts for our federal clients. You'll work with cutting-edge security tools and collaborate with a team of experienced professionals.
